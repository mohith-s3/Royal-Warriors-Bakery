About Us

WhiskAway by Titli was born out of a simple passion for baking and a big dream in the heart of Siliguri. Founded by Titli, a self-taught home baker, the brand is built on love, creativity, and the joy of making people smile through sweet indulgences. What started as a small experiment in the home kitchen quickly grew into a name people associate with warmth and celebration. Every cake at WhiskAway is made with care, using only the best ingredients and crafted to be 100% eggless, so everyone can enjoy without compromise.
